# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026

Dot to Dust is designed as a local-only app. It does not require an account, does not sell data, and does not include advertising, analytics, or cloud sync operated by Dot to Dust.

## Information Stored by the App

Dot to Dust stores only the preferences needed to run the app:

- date of birth
- theme preference
- default grid view

This information is stored locally on your device using the app's private storage.

## Information We Collect

Dot to Dust does not collect, transmit, or sell your date of birth or app preferences.

The app does not include:

- accounts or sign-in
- advertising SDKs
- analytics SDKs
- in-app purchases
- developer-operated cloud sync
- remote user profiles

## Device Backups

Your device operating system may include Dot to Dust's local app data in normal device backups, such as iCloud backup on iOS or Android backup on Android. Those backups are provided by your device platform, not by Dot to Dust.

## App Updates

Dot to Dust may contact app update services, including the Apple App Store, Google Play, and Expo update infrastructure, to install or check for app updates. These services may process technical information such as device type, app version, operating system version, IP address, and update request metadata according to their own privacy policies.

Dot to Dust does not use those update requests to build user profiles or track your use of the app.

## Permissions

Dot to Dust does not request access to contacts, location, photos, camera, microphone, health data, calendars, reminders, or notifications.

## Data Sharing

Dot to Dust does not share your date of birth or preferences with third parties.

Platform providers and update services may process technical data as described in their own privacy policies when you install, update, back up, or restore the app.

## Data Deletion

You can delete Dot to Dust's local data by deleting the app from your device. Depending on your device settings, backed-up app data may also need to be removed from your iCloud or Android backup settings.

If the app provides settings to edit your date of birth or preferences, changes are saved locally on your device.

## Children's Privacy

Dot to Dust is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when Dot to Dust changes how it handles data. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support channel listed on the App Store or Google Play listing for Dot to Dust.
